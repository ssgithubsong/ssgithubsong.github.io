# 😀 Me

👋 Hi, I'm Shuang Song, Researcher at USTB, School of Computer & Communication Engineering  

🔬 Focus: Intelligent image computing for cardiovascular & cerebrovascular diseases  

🏥 Clinical partners: Beijing Anzhen, Xuanwu, 301, Fuwai hospitals  

My research spans:  
- **Medical image analysis** (CT, MRI, angiography) for phenotyping and risk assessment  
- **Virtual intervention** – simulation and planning of endovascular procedures  
- **Bypass surgery planning** – computational support for CABG and other revascularization strategies  

I work closely with leading cardiology, neurology, and cardiac surgery centers in Beijing to develop AI-driven tools that move from imaging to treatment planning.  

📫 Open to collaborations in medical imaging, virtual intervention, surgical simulation, and clinical AI.
